Overall, behavioral analytics in cybersecurity is important on the internet because it helps organizations detect and respond to potential security threats that originate from online sources. By monitoring user behavior and identifying abnormal activity, security teams can quickly detect and respond to potential breaches and protect their networks and data from harm. With the increasing use of the internet and online services, cybercriminals are increasingly using the internet as a platform to launch attacks such as phishing, malware, and botnets.
